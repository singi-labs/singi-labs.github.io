Copy-ready descriptions

Use these as-is or adapt them. If you need something different, reach out.

Singi Labs (one-liner)

Singi Labs builds open source, decentralized software on the AT Protocol. Home of Barazo (community forums) and Sifa (professional identity and trust).

Barazo (short)

Barazo is a federated forum on the AT Protocol. Your identity and posts live on your PDS, not locked inside each community. One account across every forum, contributions follow you. Open source, self-hostable, privacy-native.

Sifa (short)

Sifa is a professional identity layer for the AT Protocol. Your profile shows verified activity from across the ecosystem: real contributions to real communities, tied to your cryptographic identity. No reputation scores, no tiers. Just what you actually did, verified.

The flywheel (for longer pieces)

Barazo and Sifa are two products that feed into each other. Activity across AT Protocol apps (and Barazo communities as they grow) builds your Sifa profile. Sifa's trust infrastructure (sybil detection, anti-abuse labels) protects Barazo communities. Neither makes full sense alone; together they create a loop that gets stronger with every community and app added to the network.
