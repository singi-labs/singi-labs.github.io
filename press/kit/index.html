Copy-ready descriptions

Use these as-is or adapt them. If you need something different, reach out.

Singi Labs (short)

Singi Labs builds open source, decentralized software on the AT Protocol. Home of Barazo Forums (federated communities) and Sifa ID (professional identity and trust).

Barazo Forums (short)

Barazo Forums is a federated forum platform on the AT Protocol. Your identity and posts live on your PDS, not locked inside each community. One account across every forum, contributions follow you. Open source, self-hostable, privacy-native.

Sifa ID (short)

Sifa ID is a professional identity layer for the AT Protocol. Your profile shows verified activity from across the ecosystem: real contributions to real communities, tied to your cryptographic identity. No reputation scores, no tiers. Just what you actually did, verified.

The flywheel (for longer pieces)

Barazo Forums and Sifa ID each work on their own. Forums are a proven model (Discourse, Flarum), and so are professional-identity networks (LinkedIn). But because both run on the AT Protocol, they reinforce each other: activity in Barazo communities builds your Sifa profile, and Sifa's trust infrastructure (sybil detection, anti-abuse labels) protects Barazo communities. Both use open lexicons, so the rest of the atproto network benefits too.
